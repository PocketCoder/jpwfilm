JakeWilliams

Writer. Director. Filmmaker.

About Me.

I'm a London based writer and filmmaker. My passion for the art started when I was younger: I'd write a short script, cast my friends and family, and then go about filming them with my phone. While unfortunately these aren't anywhere online, my more recent work is viewable here.


On: Writing.

I believe that the characters and their environments are harmonious, each affect the other and each, in turn, change over the course of the story. I focus on creating strong characters with bases in reality and fringes in the beautiful world of the cinematic.

On: Directing.

My vision when directing is to stay true to the subjects and their worlds, while elevating them to the romantic visuals cinema can create. A collabrative effort, film is the sum of a host of people and I always aim to honour that.

On: Vision.

My overarching goal, and the reason I want to create films, is to give a voice to the unheard. Mainstream cinema is flooded with stereotypes and templates, my aim is to challenge these and give the audience a new look into hidden lives.
